Our Events

What does the community have to say about us?

AWS Workshop

The seminar is to provide the insights about AWS and web applications to the students and guide them. P.V.Nageswara Rao sir; Chairperson BoS addressed students regarding Cloud technology and how the world is being run by the AWS and how this workshop will be advantageous during the placements.

Blockchain Workshop

The aim of the Blockchain Bootcamp was to introduce the Blockchain technology to the participants and hands-on workshop on Ethereum so that the participants will have a better approach for building decentralized applications. It was a certification program from DCentrum Community.

Technical Talk on Networking

The technical talk was conducted to enlighten the student’s minds and guide them through the wisdom and knowledge. focused that Technology, Communication, and Integrity are the three important domains a student must be good at.

ENGINEER’S DAY KAHOOT QUIZ

The event started with 3 Preliminary Rounds in total. Students were segregated into batches for each round. The top 2 teams will be awarded with prizes on the occasion of Engineer’s Day. The event was very successful, and students showed a lot of enthusiasm to participate and compete in the event.

IEEE-CS Engineer's Day

The Engineer’s Day event was held to celebrate the Birth Anniversary of Shri.M.Visvesvaraya. Amidst this celebration our IEEE-CS winner and runner-up for the Engineer's Day Kahoot Quiz event were awarded with prizes from our honorable President Shri.M.Sri Bharat.

Orientation Event

This session covered the benefits of joining and working in IEEE_CS, Each and every detail was clearly depicted to students! A Q&A session, at last, cleared all the doubts from students, A count of 100+ participants joined the session with the Current Ex-Com team of IEEE_CS.

Study Abroad-Way

The seminar is to enlighten the student minds and guide them for pursuing higher studies abroad. Mr.K.P. Singh spoke about profile building with soft skills alongside discussed the courses and educational programme of foreign universities.
